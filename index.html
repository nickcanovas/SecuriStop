Our Purpose

Securistop exists to protect children by delivering the most accurate, secure, and enforceable stop-arm safety system in the nation. Our mission is to support law enforcement with reliable, court-ready evidence and to deter violations before they become tragedies.

Securistop was founded to stop preventable harm. We build enforcement technology that gives officers the tools they need to hold dangerous drivers accountable and to ensure every school bus is protected with secure, verifiable, and defensible evidence.
