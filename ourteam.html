Meet the Team

Jorge Canovas, CEO & Co-founder

With a distinguished 25-year career in global compliance and strategic leadership, Jorge Canovas brings deep expertise and vision to his role. Jorge's background includes senior roles at industry-leading organizations and a strong foundation in legal and regulatory frameworks. His leadership style emphasizes integrity, operational excellence, and fortifying the company's national presence. At the helm of our organization, Jorge is committed to driving our strategic growth agenda, fostering a culture of accountability and innovation, and ensuring that we deliver on our promise of excellence to clients, partners, and stakeholders.

Nick Canovas, CTO & Co-founder

With a background in aerospace engineering and technology leadership, Nick Canovas brings extensive experience to the role of CTO. In his previous position, he worked as an aerospace engineer at NASA, where he contributed to research for advanced propulsion system techology and aircraft vehicle design. Nick has also previously worked for various engineering firms across industry and government, driving innovation in system design, software architecture, and mission-critical hardware along the way. In his current role, Nick leads our technology roadmap, combining his deep multi-disciplinary systems engineering expertise with a vision for scalable, high-impact solutions. He's committed to leveraging aerospace-grade rigor and industry best practices to build the next generation of products and platforms - and to empower our engineering teams to solve hard problems with clarity and creativity.
