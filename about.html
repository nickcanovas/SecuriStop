Taking a Technology-Driven Approach to Safety and Deterrence

We believe our technology has the power to deter school bus traffic violations before they happen. Our team is made up of forward-thinking technologists and experienced leaders, united by a shared mission: protecting children across the country by making roads safer for every school bus.

In a nutshell, SecuriStop is built to deliver on these key objectives:

  1. Prevent Violations: Deploy intelligent detection systems and signage to discourage and document illegal school bus passing.
  2. Support Law Enforcement: Provide accurate, timely data to assist enforcement agencies in addressing violations effectively.
  3. Enhance Community Safety: Increase driver accountability through technology that prioritizes child safety.
  4. Promote Awareness: Drive long-term behavioral change by making safety visible and measurable.
